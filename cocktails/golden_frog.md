# Golden Frog

## Ingredients

* 1 oz    vodka
* 0.75 oz lemon juice
* 0.5 oz  Galliano 
* 0.5 oz  Strega

## Preparation

Shake, serve up.

***

## Notes

Sources appear to disagree on this recipe quite a bit. For example, there's [this recipe](https://www.liquor.com/recipes/golden-frog/#gs.s49p07) on liquor.com like this:

* 0.75 oz vodka
* 0.75 oz Galliano
* 0.75 oz falernum
* 0.75 oz lemon juice
* 1       egg white
