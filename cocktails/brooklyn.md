# Brooklyn

## Ingredients

* 2 oz rye
* 1 oz sweet vermouth
* 0.25 oz Maraschino
* 0.25 oz Amer Picon (or a few dashes of Angostura orange bitters)

## Preparation

Stir with ice, serve up.

***

## Notes

Amer Picon is hard to find in North America. Ramazzotti amaro will do a decent job in place of it as well.
