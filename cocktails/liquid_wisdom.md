Liquid Wisdom

1.5 oz  chamomile-infused scotch
0.5 oz  Belle de Brillet
0.5 oz  honey syrup
0.75 oz lemon juice
1 dash  simple syrup
1 dash  Spanish bitters

Shake, serve up.
