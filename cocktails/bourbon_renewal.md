# Bourbon Renewal

## Ingredients

* 2 oz   bourbon
* 1 oz   lemon juice
* 0.5 oz creme de cassis
* 0.5 oz simple syrup
* 1 dash Angostura bitters

## Preparation

Shake with ice, then strain over fresh ice. Garnish with a lemon wedge.
