Golden Frog

1 oz    vodka
0.75 oz lemon juice
0.5 oz  Galliano 
0.5 oz  Strega

Shake, serve up.

--------------------

Sources appear to disagree on this recipe quite a bit. For example, there's a recipe on liquor.com like this:

0.75 oz vodka
0.75 oz Galliano
0.75 oz falernum
0.75 oz lemon juice
1       egg white
