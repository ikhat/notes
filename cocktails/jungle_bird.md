# Jungle Bird

## Ingredients

* 1.5 oz  blackstrap rum
* 0.75 oz Campari
* 0.5 oz  lime juice
* 0.5 oz  simple syrup
* 1.5 oz  pineapple juice

## Preparation

Shake with ice, then strain into an old fashioned glass over fresh ice (ideally crushed ice). 

Optimally garnished with a slice of pineapple.
